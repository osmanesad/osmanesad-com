About

Osman Esad

Designer, developer, and publisher based in Istanbul. This page is a simple index for ongoing work.

I work across interfaces, small tools, and visual experiments. Most of my projects live in three places: writing, art, and code. Instead of building a complex portfolio, I’m keeping this space as a quiet hub that points to where things are actually happening.

I enjoy long-term projects, slow iterations, and documenting process. When possible, I prefer to ship small, focused pieces rather than big “launches”.

If you’d like to collaborate, talk about work, or just say hello, you can reach me via email. I’m open to remote collaborations, experimental projects, and carefully scoped client work.
